Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

85% of your donation goes directly to program activities, including school construction, teacher training, and educational materials. Only 15% is used for administrative and fundraising costs.

Can I sponsor a specific student?

Yes! Our student sponsorship program allows you to sponsor a specific child's education for $500 per year. You'll receive regular updates on their progress.

Where are the schools located?

We operate schools in underserved communities across multiple countries. You can specify a region when making your donation if you have a preference.

How can I volunteer with this program?

We welcome volunteers with teaching experience, construction skills, or a passion for education. Visit our volunteer page to learn more and apply.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Charity Hope is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records.
